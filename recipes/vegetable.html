Vegetable soup



DESCRIPTION

Vegetable soup is a native to the yoruba people of nigeria. It has a lot of health benefits, it is known as EFO ELEMI MEJE among yoruba people. It can be eaten with swallows, rice, yam and so on.


INGREDIENTS

  • Vegetable leaf, chopped
  • Vegetable oil
  • onion
  • garlic
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound cow skin(POMO)
  • seasoning
  • 2 teaspoons salt (optional)
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger
  • meat stock
  • Fish

STEPS

  1. Heat enough vegetable oil in a pot
  2. Blend tomatoes, onions, pepper, Garlic, and Ginger
  3. pour the blended mixture into the heated vegetable oil, stir until properly fried
  4. Add seasoning and salt to taste
  5. Cut the cow skin into little bits. add meat stock, Fish, cooked beef, and cow skin
  6. Rince the chopped vegetable leaf
  7. add the chopped vegetable leaf, and allow to cook for 25 minutes

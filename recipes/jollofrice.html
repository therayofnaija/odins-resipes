Nigerian jollof rice



DESCRIPTION

Nigerian jollof rice is a delicacy that is most enjoyed by nigerians. It is very common among all tribes in Nigeria and it is an important part of any kind of event in Nigeria.


INGREDIENTS

  • pepper
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Tomato paste
  • Uncooked rice
  • Groundnut oil
  • Seasoning
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Bay leaf
  • Meat stock

STEPS

  1. Heat enough vegetable oil in a pot, and add sliced onions. Allow to fry for some miutes.
  2. Blend tomatoes, pepper, Garlic, Ginger, and onions. Add the mixture into the fried onions, stir until well fried.
  3. Add seasoning and salt
  4. Add meat stock
  5. Add your washed rice, and add Bay leaf
  6. Allow to cook for 30 minutes
  7. Stir gently with spatula and reduce the heat
  8. Allow to cook for an extra 5 minutes
